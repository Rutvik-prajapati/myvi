MyVI
Objective:
* Users can online recharge their VI(Vodafone,Idea) sim card.
* Buy a new VI sim card online and order it.


Website Role:
* Users(public,Administrator) 

Admin:
* Register
* Login
* Plan Type manage like.. Prepaid plan, Postpaid plan etc.. 
* Recharge Plan Create,Update,Delete
* VIP number Create,Update,Delete
* Manage User Orders
        
User:
* Register
* Login
* Recharge Their mobile number
* Get all Plans(able to see all recharge plan)
* Buy new sim card(able to order)
* Other Company SIM Port into VI
* Choose VIP number (order it)
